DEPENDENT VARIABLE: Reaction time of a Person.

INDEPENDENT VARIABLE: Colour of ink(congruent and incongruent condition)

HYPOTHESIS: H0(Null hypothesis) --- No significant difference in time on reading the congruent and incongruent i.e. mu(congruent) = mu(incongruent) ha(Alternative hypothesis) --- Significant difference is there i.e. mu(congruent) > mu(incongruent) mu(congruent) < mu(incongruent) mu(congruent) not equals mu(incongruent)

HYPOTHESIS PERFORMED: A two tailed t-test WHY THIS TEST??? This test is performed as we are comparing the means of two conditions(of congruency and incongruency) against reaction time. Also the stdev of population is unknown, that's why we are not using the z-score. The sample size is less than 30. The distributions are normal.
